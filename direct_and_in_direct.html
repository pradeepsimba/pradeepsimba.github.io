Direct speech :

To introduce the speaker's words, there should be a comma (,) after 'said'.

Indirect speech :

* Indirect speech is usually used to talk about the past because of that the actual words have been spoken by the speaker in the past, so to narrate it in

present, it requires to change in tense .

* The word "that" need to add before the speaker's words.

Reporting verb VS Reported speech :

Reporting verb

* we use reporting verbs like 'say','tell','ask'.

* The verb of the first sentence is called reporting verb, such as 'said','says','say'.Etc.

Reporting speech :

* The second sentence which is enclosed in inverted commas is called reported speech.

Eg : she said, “I am going to school”.
